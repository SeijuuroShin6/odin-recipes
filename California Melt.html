California Melt

A satisfying open-face vegetarian sandwich with avocado, almonds and mushrooms.

Ingredients needed for the recipe

  • 4 Slices whole-grain bread, lightly toasted.
  • 1 Avocado, sliced.
  • 1 Cup sliced Mushrooms.
  • â…“ Cup sliced toasted Almonds.
  • 1 Tomato sliced.
  • 4 Slices Swiss Cheese.
  1. Step 1 : Preheat the oven broiler.
  2. Step 2 : Lay the toasted bread out on a baking sheet. Top each slice of bread with 1/4 of the avocado, mushrooms, almonds, and tomato slices. Top each with a slice of Swiss cheese.
  3. Step 3 : Broil the open-face sandwiches until the cheese melts and begins to bubble, about 2 minutes. Serve the sandwiches warm.
PS : Please note the addition of a basil-mayonnaise (1/4 cup mayonnaise mixed with 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil) if using the magazine version of this recipe.
