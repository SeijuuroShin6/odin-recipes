Moroccan Breakfast Skillet

Recipe Description

Moroccan-inspired ingredients combine for ultimate flavor in this quick and easy breakfast skillet dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Swiss chard, or more to taste
  • 8 Ounces ground turkey
  • 1 (8 ounce) Package bulk pork breakfast sausage (such as Bob Evans®)
  • 1 Medium sweet potato, diced
  • 3 Cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Teaspoon Ground turmeric
  • ½ Teaspoon Sea Salt
  • ½ Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 1 Teaspoon Apple Cider Vinegar

Steps to follow for making the recipe

  1. Step 1 : Remove stems from Swiss chard. Chop stems and leaves, keeping in separate bowls.
  2. Step 2 : Place turkey and sausage into the bottom of a cold, heavy-bottomed pan and break up slightly using a utensil. Heat over medium-high heat and cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Turn off heat, transfer meat to a large bowl using a slotted spoon, and leave fat in the pan.
  3. Step 3 : Return pan back to the stovetop over medium-high heat. Add sweet potato and cook and stir for 5 minutes. Add chard stems and cook for 3 minutes more.
  4. Step 4 : Add garlic, turmeric, sea salt, and cinnamon to the pan; stir to combine. Cook until sweet potatoes are just soft, about 5 minutes. Add chard leaves and apple cider vinegar. Continue cooking until greens have wilted, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from heat, mix in cooked meat, and serve warm.
